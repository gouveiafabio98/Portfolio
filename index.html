#001 Ciclo de Conversas Design + Multimédia

This is an academic project within 'Project 1 - Web Identity' unit at the University of Coimbra, where we had to create a visual identity of the event 'Ciclo de Conversas - Design e Multimédia' that takes place in Coimbra, Portugal every year.

Our identity has been chosen to represent the event in 2020. We created a square divided in two, which forms the two C's in the initials of the event (CCDM), to match the square we created a modular typography and we wrote the remaining letters of the initials with this.

Our logo adapts to all formats like posters, computers, phones, banners, etc., and with this potencial we could wrote all the information needed inside of it.
